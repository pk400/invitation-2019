So how do I play?

The world of D&D exists in the theatre of the mind. What does that mean? It means to use the side of the brain that has been dormant for the last decade. Just kidding.

A quick high level overview of the game. There are two types of players: the DM (Dungeon Master), and just regular players. The DM's are in charge of narrating the story, telling you what is currently happening in the world and to enforce a specific set of rules (like a referee), and it is up to you (the player) to decide what you want to do. But you must be wondering... Okay, so what can I do? In terms of interacting with the world, you can do anything! You simply tell the DM what you want to do, roll some dice, and they will tell you how your character does it. A PDF containing the Basic Rules can be found here. Feel free to ask your DM for any questions you may have!

Tell me more!

Uh.. Okay..So each player gets to choose (pre-generated or player-created) a character that they wish to play as. This character is such a large part of the game that it is often overlooked for newcomers to the game. If you could be anyone other than yourself who would you be (within the context of the realm of course).

Where did your character grow up, what are their personality quirks, what is that weird thing they like to do, are they impulsive, are they cunning, do they have a silver tongue, are they seeking fame, are they willing to sacrifice themselves for the sake of others, are they from a noble family, or are they born with no last name? These are some questions to ask yourself when thinking of a character. The best way to imagine it is, is to create a character that you would love to see in Lord of the Rings or any similar movie.

I will of course provide pre-generated characters for those who are looking for a more casual experience. But even with a pre-generated character feel free to be as creative as you would like.

Home Rules

More detailed rules will be explained on the day of the event, but I hope the summary above is enough to get you excited to play (I got excited typing this lol)! Below are some home rules that I would like us to abide by when playing (so things don't get too out of hand):

  1. Don't try killing other players.
  2. We will be following a module, so let's try to stay on track of the main storyline.
  3. ROLEPLAY! (This makes it more fun for me too)
  4. But don't try seducing the character the DM is playing. (Just..no)
  5. Be a team player. Solve problems together and let others have a chance in the spotlight.
  6. DON'T SPLIT THE PARTY.
  7. Lots of rules will be very loose, so no pro-gamers allowed.
  8. No meta-gaming. (Don't look up the module ahead of time)
  9. Be kind to your fellow gamer.

That's about it!

The last and most important rule would be to Have fun, and enjoy the adventure. We are getting too old to be bitter about the little things in life (I'm turning 26 dammit). Let's enjoy the time spent with the other people in our lives and share one of the greatest stories ever told.
